Our Story

Cooking with heart
since 2025

Hotel Raj Palace started as a humble paratha stall near Jyotirao Phule Circle, Alwar. Today we're a beloved destination for families who crave the warmth of home-cooked food made with honest ingredients.

Every paratha is rolled by hand, cooked on a tawa over slow heat, and finished with a generous ladle of pure desi ghee — nothing more, nothing less.
